About Kirav
Food Tech

Kirav Food Tech is a trusted name in food flavour manufacturing and distribution. We provide premium flavours and ingredients to help food and beverage brands deliver outstanding taste and aroma.

Based in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, we partner with India's and the world's leading flavour houses — including IFF, Sensient, Bell, Döhler and more.

To be a leading flavour partner for the food industry by offering creative, safe, and high-quality taste solutions that inspire every bite.
